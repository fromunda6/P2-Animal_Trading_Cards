Great Horned Owl

The eyes of great horned owls are amongst the largest and most powerfully acute in the animal kingdom.

  • Scientific Name: Bubo virginianus
  • Average Wingspan: 122 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 13 years
  • Habitat: Western Hemisphere, all subactic zones excluding the tropics

The most widely-distributed true owl in the Americas, the Great Horned Owl freely hunts any animal it can overtake. Colored for camouflage, this is one formidable hunter!
